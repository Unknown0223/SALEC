// Claude.ai ga paste qiling Distribyutsiya savdo tizimi uchun multi-tenant (diler/filial) arxitekturasi bilan Prisma schema yoz. KONTEKST: - Biz distribyutsiya kompaniyalari uchun SaaS tizim yasayapmiz - Har diler (kompaniya) = bitta tenant - Bir diler ma'lumotlarini boshqa diler ko'ra olmaydi - Bitta PostgreSQL database, shared schema - Har jadvalda tenant_id ustuni bo'ladi KERAKLI JADVALLAR: 1. tenants (dilerlar ro'yxati): id, slug (unique: 'andijon', 'namangan'...), name, phone, address, logo_url, plan (enum: basic/pro/enterprise), is_active, created_at, settings JSON (bonus_enabled, gps_enabled, max_users, default_credit_limit) 2. tenant_features (har dilerga alohida modul): id, tenant_id, feature_name, is_enabled 3. users (foydalanuvchilar): id, tenant_id, name, login, password_hash, role (enum: admin/supervisor/agent/warehouse/delivery), is_active, last_login 4. clients (mijozlar): id, tenant_id, name, address, phone, territory_id, agent_id, category, credit_limit, initial_balance, gps_lat, gps_lng, is_active 5. products (mahsulotlar): id, tenant_id (null = barcha dilerlarga umumiy), sku, name, category_id, unit, barcode, is_active 6. orders (zakazlar): id, tenant_id, number (tenant ichida unikal), client_id, agent_id, warehouse_id, status, total_sum, bonus_sum, discount_sum, payment_type, created_at 7. warehouses (omborlar): id, tenant_id, name, type, address, is_active 8. stock (qoldiqlar): id, tenant_id, warehouse_id, product_id, qty, reserved_qty 9. payments (to'lovlar): id, tenant_id, client_id, order_id, amount, payment_type, is_confirmed, created_at 10. gps_logs (GPS): id, tenant_id, user_id, lat, lng, recorded_at Har jadvalda tenant_id majburiy (null bo'lmasin). Composite index: (tenant_id, ...) — tez so'rov uchun. Prisma schema formatida yoz. Relations to'liq.
